Things to do in Buffalo

The Beautiful Outdoors

Buffalo has numerous outdoor activities to keep you busy, including the Olmsted Park System, which is comprised of 6 parks, 7 parkways, 8 circles and several smaller spaces. The Buffalo Olmsted Park System is our nation's first system of parks, parkways and circles, and was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
